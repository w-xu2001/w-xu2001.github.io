[PointSLAM++: Robust Dense Neural Gaussian Point Cloud-based SLAM]() \\
**Xu Wang**, Boyao Han, Xiaojun Chen, Ying Liu, Ruihui Li.

By jointly leveraging geometric and feature-based constraints and adaptive Gaussian anchors, the system achieves superior robustness to depth noise and state-of-the-art performance on large-scale indoor scenes.

[MGS-SLAM: Dense RGB-D SLAM via Multi-level Gaussian Splatting.](https://diglib.eg.org/handle/10.2312/pg20241274) \\
**Xu Wang**, Ying Liu, Xiaojun Chen, Jialin Wu, Xiaohao Zhang, Ruihui Li

<!-- [**Project**](https://speechresearch.github.io/fastspeech2/) <strong><span class='show_paper_citations' data='4FA6C0AAAAAJ:LkGwnXOMwfcC'></span></strong> -->

- This work proposes MGS-SLAM, a progressive Gaussian splatting based SLAM framework that unifies camera tracking and dense mapping within a multi-scale neural Gaussian representation. The key idea is to progressively optimize Gaussian primitives from coarse geometry to fine details, which improves robustness to depth noise and enhances reconstruction quality. The system achieves accurate localization and photorealistic 3D mapping in real time, outperforming prior Gaussian-based SLAM approaches on multiple benchmarks.
